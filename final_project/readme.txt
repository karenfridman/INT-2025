Final project for INT Data Science Course 2025
CNN based on EfficientNetB0 to recognize 4 types of blood cells (neutrophil, eosinophil, lymphocyte or monocyte)

The folder contains:
  main.py
  blood_app.py
  best_4class_blood_model_imp.keras
  requirements.txt
  train_history.pkl
